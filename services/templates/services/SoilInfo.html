Soil Types & Suitable Crops

Loamy Soil

Balanced mixture of sand, silt, and clay. Good drainage and moisture retention.

  • Wheat
  • Cotton
  • Sugarcane
  • Oilseeds
  • Vegetables

Clay Soil

Fine particles, holds water well, heavy and poorly drained.

  • Rice
  • Lettuce
  • Broccoli
  • Cabbage
  • Kale

Sandy Soil

Large particles, drains quickly, warms up fast in spring.

  • Watermelons
  • Peanuts
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions

Silt Soil

Fine, smooth soil that retains moisture but drains slowly.

  • Rice
  • Wheat
  • Barley
  • Cotton
  • Vegetables
